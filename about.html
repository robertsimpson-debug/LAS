Professional Profile

I’m the Director of Instruction at Springfield Golf & Country Club in Virginia, where I lead instruction, junior programming, and academy standards across private lessons and group development pathways.

I also own TGA of Fairfax, a youth sports company delivering golf, tennis, pickleball, and soccer programs through school and community partnerships across Northern Virginia.

  • Coaching focus: ball flight control, scoring skills, and practice structure that produces on-course results.
  • Junior development: clear levels, measurable benchmarks, and pathways into competitive play.
  • Adult development: strategy, wedges, short game, and course management built around your goals.
  • Program building: academy systems that scale while keeping coaching quality consistent.
